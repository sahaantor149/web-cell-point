Purchase management

Purchasing raw materials for production is one of the major parts of the Agro-Feed manufacturing industry. And its important to make all the raw materials available before starting production.

Moreover, this module is directly integrated with the account management module as in whenever & whatever raw maternal or chemical purchases done by the Agro-Feed will be recorded in the accounting module. And this purchase module will record all the purchase details, when it was purchased, and also keep track of the expiry date; the quantity ordered, etc.

Although there are three types of procurement & purchase module system types: local, foreign, and direct purchase, this purchase and procurement management module will include the following:

  • Raw material order & received
  • Suppliers details
  • Receipt voucher
