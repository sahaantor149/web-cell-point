Full Package of Some Startling Modules for Your Curtain Business!!!

Are you curious to know which modules you will win from this window covering software? Now, it's time to reveal all of these, and here you will get a detailed list of all modules that we offer.

Factual Dashboard

All of your business aspects are reflected in your dashboard. Thus, it's mandatory to have a dynamic dashboard that could be able to capture all real-time information. In this module, you can see the total number of customers, suppliers, new purchase product quantity, number of pending invoices and orders, new orders, and manufacturing quantity. Also, the monthly sales amount and sales order is represented graphically. With this module, you will get your current sales report according to the year, month, week, and day.

  • Monthly order & sales amount
  • Current sales report
  • Complete order
  • Daily, weekly, monthly, yearly sales report

CRM System

Without a proper customer handling system, your business will be completely paralyzed. Customers are the key asset of your business because they drive revenue in your organization. If you demand a resilient business, you will definitely need a complete customer management system.

This module can be able to bring about your assertion because you can add new customers by filling out the name and other information. Also, you can get a list of your customers that will aid you to manage them properly.

  • Add customer
  • Manage customer

Order Management

This one is another essential module for any blinds, shades, and shutters businesses. In this module, the order receiving process is done by completing three steps. The customers need to add the product to the cart, and then it will need to fill up the required customer information. After that, this module will show the order details.

Here, you will get another exclusive option that can be able to generate a list of all orders that will be very effective in understanding the detailed information of each order. With this module, you can generate a unique order ID and barcode to ensure your system's security and safety.

  • New order
  • Manage order
  • Order details

Catalog Management

In the curtain management software, you will get a catalog management system that can allow you to add several categories, manage those categories. In this module, you will get a pattern option that helps you to differentiate the variation according to product size, color, price, and so on.

When you add any product, you must have to select the pattern because you have the same product in different variations, but the price will not be the same. This module can also allow you to add product attributes that will reflect the characteristics of your products, such as size, color, weight, volume, and so on.

  • Add category
  • Manage category
  • Manage pattern
  • Manage color
  • Add attribute
  • Manage attribute

Pricing Model

Proper pricing helps to improve your sales; as a result, your business revenue will be increased. For this reason, you must need to follow an effective pricing strategy. This software provides two types of pricing models: the Row Column Price model and the Group Price model.

Your entire cash flow largely depends on which pricing model you will follow and how effectively it will work. Also, this module can allow you to map your group price based on your product pattern. So, it will be very profitable for your business.

  • Row Column Price
  • Group Price
  • Group price mapping

Product Management

This software provides an effective product management system. This software is designed to consider those who want to deal with blinds, shades, and shutters business. So, it's crucial to ensure all of your resources. You can keep track and store your products through this module and add new products according to the category and pricing model.

Moreover, it will be significant to ensure proper product management for providing the best service to your customers. With this module, you can accumulate all of your product handling tasks, such as add and manage products, manage raw materials, and many more.

  • Add product
  • Manage product
  • Purchase management
  • Manage raw materials

Production System

Production is an essential process that can make sure the maximum output from a certain amount of input. Therefore, you need an automation system to maintain the overall process effectively. For the curtain management business, you have to ensure enough flow of your raw materials, and you need to manage your manufacturing process.

With this module, you can handle all production information. Also, this module can allow you to manage your raw materials and usages of raw materials. Here, you can conduct the manufacturing process and add manufacturing information through this module.

  • Manage manufacturing
  • Raw material usage
  • Supplier management

Stock Management

The stock management system is another exclusive module that can help to understand the current situation of your inventory. You will get this module in the curtain management software.

With this module, you can check the product availability, and you can easily observe total in quantity, out quantity, and available quantity. So, you can quickly determine which products will need to be restocked and which products are enough availability to sell.

  • Stock availability
  • Stock details

Returning System

This software allows a return option because this module is essential to gather the customer's satisfaction and the owner as well. After purchasing any products, in case of any logical reason, customers can return your products.

As well as, you can also return any product for any logical reason to the supplier. With this module, you can get these two types of returning facilities and manage the rest of the processes systematically.

  • Customer return
  • Supplier return: Raw materials

Account Management

The account management system is a common but crucial module for all business management software. This window covering software provides a complete chart of accounts, comprehensive account reports, and several vouchers. With this module, you can keep a record of your assets, equity, expense, income, and liabilities.

There is a voucher option that will generate several types of vouchers, such as a debit voucher, credit voucher, contra voucher, and journal voucher. This module can generate the overall financial reports, including bank book, cash book, cash flow, general ledger, profit loss, and trial balance.

  • Chart of Accounts (COA)
  • Account reports
  • Several vouchers
  • Voucher reports
